GROWTH PATH

ShARE-UP offers a structured growth path for all members, guiding them through meaningful roles and learning opportunities within our organization.

Junior Member

As a Junior Member, you are introduced to one of the four Networks, where you will develop a research project and familiarize yourself with ShARE's guidelines and templates. Each network focuses on a specific area of study:

Energy

“The environmental and sustainability study”

Technology

“The technological and scientific study”

Global Economy

“The economic, business, and humanitarian study”

The City

“The process, industry, and urban study”

Confirmed Member

As a Confirmed Member, you participate in the “Learning Unit Project” and take on an in-depth consulting project for a company through an “In-House Project.” At this stage, you can also explore joining one of ShARE-UP's internal departments:

Human Resources

Corporate Relations

Marketing

Senior Member and Beyond

Upon reaching Senior Member status, you are officially recognized as a “Do Well Do Good Leader” and are encouraged to continue developing your skills while contributing to the organization’s mission. This stage allows you to gain leadership experience and work on advanced projects.
